Stay Organized, Together – with Communitly!

Communitly is a fresh way to grow, connect, and accomplish your goals. Whether you’re looking to start a blog, get fit, manage finances, or just meet like-minded people—you’ll find support inside focused micro-groups of 5–10 people.

🎯 Why Communitly?

✔️ Small – 5 to 10 members max
✔️ Purposeful – Goal-focused and time-limited (30 days)
✔️ Supportive – Chat, track tasks, and cheer each other on

🔑 Core Features

✅ Create or Join Micro-Groups – Set your goal and team up with others on the same mission.
✅ 30-Day Challenge Structure – Enough time to make real progress without burnout.
✅ Real-Time Group Chat – Stay connected, share wins, and support each other daily.
✅ Simple Matchmaking – We’ll suggest groups that align with your goals or interests.
✅ Task Tracking – Set and monitor progress with accountability tools.
✅ Personalized Profile – Highlight your goals, skills, and progress.
✅ Freemium Model – Free: Join 1 group. Pro ($5/month): Unlimited groups + smarter matching.
