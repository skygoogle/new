Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08/07/25

Communitly respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data Collected

We collect the following data:

Personal data (linked to your identity):

  • Name
  • E-mail address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Physical address
  • Financial Data: Payment information (collected only after trial period)
  • Identifiers: User ID
  • Location (If you want)

Data not linked to your identity:

  • Technical Data: Error logs, crash data, performance data, diagnostic data (in case of issues)

2. Use of Data

The data collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Billing and Payment Management
  • Renewal of subscriptions
  • Bug and technical issue analysis
  • Improved application performance

3. Sharing of Data

We share certain data with third parties to ensure the proper functioning of our services:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze the performance of our service.
  • Apple and Google: For in-app payments and diagnostic data.

We limit data sharing to what is strictly necessary. You will be notified of significant changes.

4. Data Security

All personal data is encrypted and stored securely. We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

5. User Rights

You have the following rights under applicable laws:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request rectification or deletion

To exercise your rights, contact us at: admin@communitly.app
Requests for data deletion are processed within 24 hours.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data for up to one year after your relationship with us ends, unless legally required to keep it longer.

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We do not use cookies in the app. Cookies may be used on our landing page to analyze traffic and enhance the user experience.

8. Legal Basis for Processing

We collect and use your personal data with your consent, which is given by creating an account and accepting this Privacy Policy.
